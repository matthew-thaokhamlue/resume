I’m a Technical Product Manager who loves solving messy problems. For the past 5+ years, I’ve been building and improving B2B SaaS products in health and lab software.

My background isn't typical. I started in Economics, which gave me a strong foundation in data, incentives, and systems thinking. This naturally evolved into a passion for technology as I saw how digital tools could transform industries.

I care deeply about collaboration, clarity, and delivering things that actually make users smile. Whether it's streamlining a lab workflow or integrating complex APIs, I focus on the human on the other side of the screen.

When I'm not building products, you'll find me exploring the culinary world. I believe that cooking shares a lot with product management: it requires attention to detail, iterative improvement, and a deep desire to create a delightful experience for others.
