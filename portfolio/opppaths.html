Why this exists

Scholarships change lives. Finding them shouldn’t take luck.

In 2018 a DAAD scholarship changed the direction of my life: it took me from Bangkok to Berlin and into the career I have now. Winning it meant navigating scattered listings, opaque requirements, and application advice that mostly costs money students don’t have.

Opportunity Paths makes that path affordable. It matches a student’s profile to scholarships, shows the gaps between where they are and what a scholarship asks for, drafts the application documents with them, and rehearses the interview, in English and Thai.

How it was built

One human, a team of agents.

I built Opportunity Paths as the sole human owner, working through a personal multi-agent AI workflow I designed: agents draft, I review and decide. The same operating system I use to run my PM job built a production web product alongside it.

The platform is live with an early-access waitlist, shipped from first commit to production configuration in six months of nights and weekends.

  • Shipped roughly 396 commits in six months as the sole human owner.
  • Localized in English and Thai, aimed first at students in the Mekong region.
  • Live at opppaths.com with an early-access waitlist.
